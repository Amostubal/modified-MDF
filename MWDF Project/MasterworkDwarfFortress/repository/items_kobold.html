Items

Kobolds are too small to use most weapons. Not a single hammer, pike or crossbow can be wielded by the average bold. Most weapons invaders and caravans bring have to be melted down and remade into kobold weapons.

Their armor options are somewhat limited in comparison to other races too.

Digger

Nothing, you dont have access to a pick, therefore: No Mining!

Weapons

Hatchet (Axe) ::: A small axe to chop wood.

Machete (Sword) ::: A small cutting weapon.

Mallet (Hammer) ::: A small blunt weapon.

Club (Mace) ::: A blunt weapon. Good against armor.

Whip (Whip) ::: A blunt lashing weapon.
